Featured Commentators

Peter Dury

Famous for his poetic and emotional style, Peter Drury adds a dramatic flair to his commentary. He is known for beautifully capturing the significance of key moments, making him a favorite among football fans worldwide.

Martin Tyler

A legendary voice in football, Martin Tyler has been commentating for over 40 years. Known for his iconic calls, such as the "Aguerooooo" moment in 2012, Tyler has been the voice behind some of the most memorable moments in football history.

Clive Tyldesley

As one of the UK's most well-known commentators, Clive Tyldesley has worked on numerous World Cups, UEFA Champions League finals, and major football events. His calm and knowledgeable delivery makes him a trusted voice in the sport.

Jon Champion

A seasoned commentator with years of experience in both domestic and international football, Jon Champion is known for his insightful analysis and calm yet engaging style. He has been a regular on major broadcasts, including ESPN and ITV.

Ray Hudson

Known for his passionate and often eccentric style, Ray Hudson brings a unique energy to football commentary. His colorful metaphors and enthusiastic reactions, especially during La Liga matches, have made him a cult figure in the football world.
